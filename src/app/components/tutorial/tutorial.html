Getting Started

Welcome to Buckets, a simple budgeting app. We take principles from zero-based budgeting and help you apply them practically. This app is currently in development, but you are welcome to test it out for free.

Click next to get started.

Bucket Types

There are two different bucket types to help you organize your transactions.

Monthly Budget

The monthly budget bucket allows limit spending and track where your money is going.

Savings Goal

The savings goal bucket gives you a space to set money aside for future purchases.

Transactions

We do not currently sync transactions with your accounts. Adding transactions manually will help you keep closer tabs on your spending.
